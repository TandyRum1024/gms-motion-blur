Introduction

Motion blur was one of the many visual effects that inspired me to learn about Shaders.
Lately, I've been experimenting with visual effects (particularly blurring & procedural animations) a lot.. Combining that with camera's motion informations, I was able to create the effect what I call camera based motion blurring effect. And I made two small projects for (mainly fun and) demonstration purposes.
In this document (post?), I'll be explaining things about my effect.
